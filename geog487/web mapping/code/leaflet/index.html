Climate Change Factors and Reservoir Water Levels in Lethbridge from 2013 to 2024

?

Select a Climate Change Factor from the dropdown and click on a Reservoir (circle icon) to see it's name and current water level relative to the period of study (2013-2024). Further click on Show Graph to see a full graph of the water level at that location over time. Then move the slider to a month/year of interest to compare the data over time. Click on a Lake/River to see it's name and area.


Data was retrieved from the City of Lethbridge, Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada, and Environment Canada.
